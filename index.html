    about the project

    Once upon a time, in a land far, far away, there lived a silly dragon named Drakey. Drakey loved to dance and sing, but he was always getting himself into trouble because of his playful nature. One day, while frolicking in a nearby forest, Drakey stumbled upon a group of frogs who were trying to launch their own meme token.

    The frogs were struggling to come up with a name that would capture people's attention, so Drakey offered to help them out. He suggested they name their token after the most famous frog meme of all time - Pepe. The frogs were thrilled with the idea and decided to name their token "PepeCoin".

    But Drakey wasn't satisfied with just helping out the frogs. He wanted to create his own meme token too. So, he got to work brainstorming names and ideas for his own token. After much thought and deliberation, he decided to call it "DrakeyCoin" - a name that perfectly captured his playful and mischievous personality.
